About

Astroblast is a fast-paced, top-down space shooter where you travel to different planets, taking on waves of alien enemies and claiming new territories. Each planet presents fresh challenges, and no two playthroughs are the same.

As you progress, you'll upgrade your ship with new weapons and abilities to help you survive the increasingly tough battles. It's all about quick reflexes, smart upgrades, and pushing yourself to see how far you can go.

Astroblast is made using:

Unity, C#

Keep in mind that Astroblast is in pre-beta which means that all features aren't implemented or finalized yet.

Download Instructions
